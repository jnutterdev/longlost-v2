Small Hours (Demo)

SoundCloud

SoundCloud embed

Also on Bandcamp

This one started as a voice memo at around 2am — just a chord shape I'd stumbled onto that I didn't want to lose. I came back to it a week later expecting to find nothing there, and instead the whole thing arrived in about forty minutes.

The demo quality is intentional. I tried recording it cleaner and something got lost in the process — the slight room noise and the roughness around the edges felt like part of it. So this is the version that exists: a single take of the guitar, one pass of the vocal, minimal editing.

The title came after. It's that specific window of night that belongs entirely to itself — after the day has finished but before anything has started again. A lot of this record is going to live there, I think.
