Photography October 27, 2025

Late October, Riverside

I'd been meaning to walk this stretch for months. The river path gets quiet in late October — most people have given up on it by the time the weather turns, which is exactly when it becomes worth going.

The light does something specific in that last hour before it goes. Everything gets a little amber and a little melancholy. I took most of these in about forty minutes.
